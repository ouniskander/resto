Nothing brings people together like a good pizza and burger

Whilst pizza and hamburgers may not be the healthiest of foods you could eat, they certainly taste great! Check out these top fun facts about pizza & hamburgers – you can also scroll further down to watch the videos.

  • Pizza originated as a simple piece of flat-bread, garnished with whatever kinds of food were readily available to the Mediterranean people.

  • This made pizza a quick and convenient meal for families or workers who didn’t have time for a full meal, much the same as today!

  • Hamburgers are made of beef, not ham, and there is much debate over whether they actually originated in Hamburg.

  • However, the hamburger in its current form, with ground beef and a bun, is a decidedly American creation.
